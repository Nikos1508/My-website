Project

Simplified Coding (Sep 2022 - Present)

Simplified Coding is a programming learning website in Greek, completely free, without ads or subscriptions. Our website was created in September 2022, in Thessaloniki, Greece. Now our team consists of Odysseus, Nikos and Marios, students of secondary education in Greek schools. Nowadays, I am working on making new lessons, polishing old lessons and making small changes in the website.

Projects

Cup-o-meter

Cup-O-Mter is a contraption that counts cups from plastic bottles. It is made with the Arduino microcontroller and 3D prints. In Greece,it's very common for schools to collect plastic bottle caps, in order to recycle them and get a wheelchair for someone disabled. With this device, there is no need for a teacher to count them manually! Cap-O-Meter counts each cup thrown in it! During this project I have made the Arduino contraption and almost every 3D print.
